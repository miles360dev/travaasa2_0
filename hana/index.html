TRAVAASA HANA: MAUI'S LAST TRULY HAWAIIAN PLACE

Long considered one of the most remarkable resorts in Hawaii, Travaasa Hana (formerly known as Hotel Hana Maui) is now even better. In fact, it was voted the "No. 1 Resort in Hawaii" in Condé Nast Traveler's 2011 Reader's Choice Awards.

Located in the town of Hana on the eastern tip of Maui, Travaasa Hana is the first resort on the island to greet the rising sun. Set amid rolling grass fields and palm trees above Kaihalulu Bay, Travaasa Hana will immerse you in the real Hawaii.

HIGHLIGHTS

Travaasa Hana Experiences

From stand up paddling, coconut husking and throw net fishing, to outdoor yoga and guided meditation, there are countless things to do here. We offer five different pathways for you to explore: Adventure, Culinary, Culture, Fitness and Spa & Wellness. Explore

#1 Resort Spa in Hawaii

The Spa at Travaasa Hana was voted the "No. 1 Resort Spa in Hawaii" in Condé Nast Traveler’s 2012 Readers’ Choice Awards. A deeply relaxing sanctuary of peace and tranquility, a visit to the Spa at Travaasa Hana is sure to restore feelings of balance and wellness to you body, mind and spirit. Explore

Rooms designed with R&R in mind

The town of Hana feels like it is a world away from the rest of Maui. So it is only appropriate that our accommodations envelop you in a similar state of mind. Whether you are staying in one of our all-inclusive, luxurious cottages or checking in to one of our spacious suites , you will find the peace and quiet you deserve. Explore

Explore your palate

Travaasa is all about experience, even when it comes to dining. From our open-faced Smoked Chicken Salad Sandwich on homemade bread with locally grown sprout salad, to fish wrapped in Ti leaves and Lilikoi Mahi with Hawaiian sweet potato and ginger lime coconut sauce, every meal you enjoy with us is a reminder that life is for living. Explore

Heavenly Hana

Picturesque views of spectacular waterfalls, a beautifully rugged shoreline, and some of the best beaches on Maui can only begin to describe what awaits you in Hana. The charm of this peaceful town is the fact that little has changed over the last 20 years. It is truly the last undeveloped tropical paradise on Maui. Explore
