Age Policy
  • The Sea Ranch Cottages and Total Travaasa packages are limited to adults 16 and over.
  • Children are welcome on the Sea Ranch Cottage side of the property during family friendly periods of March 11–24, July 25–August 8, and December 22–January 5, 2013.
  • Children are welcome on the Garden View Suite side of the property throughout the year.
Stay Longer, Save More

Multiple night discounts are applied when you choose your dates.

The Fine Print
  • There is no minimum length of stay required to book an inclusive package.
  • Premium activities and private sessions may be purchased on an individual basis.
  • High season is July 1–August 31; Holiday Season is December 22–January 5, 2013.
  • An advance deposit of one night is required to guarantee your reservation. Advance deposit becomes non-refundable 10 days prior to arrival date of your reservation.
  • Rates are exclusive of applicable taxes and an 18% daily service charge. The service charge is designed to cover access to regularly scheduled experiences and classes, local phone calls, access to fitness center, resort facilities, transportation from and to the Hana Airport (HNM), valet parking and gratuities on all package components.
  • Trail rides and tasting tours at Ono Farms are an additional charge. Total Travaasa resort credits may be used toward these activities.
  • Please note: Travaasa Hana is a non-tipping resort property other than food & beverage areas.

CANCELLATION POLICY: All cancellations must be made at least 10 days before arrival date of reservation to receive refund of your advance deposit.
