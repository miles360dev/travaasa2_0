Your Maui vacation is not just about the destination, but also about the journey getting there. The Road to Hana is one of the most beautiful drives in the world with over 620 curves, 59 bridges suspended over lush rainforest terrain and some of the most breathtaking views. The Hana Highway is filled with roadside stops and must-see attractions. If road trips aren’t your style, we also offer flights from Maui to Hana for a bird’s eye view. more
