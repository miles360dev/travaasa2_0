THIS MONTH

Dive in and drink up.

One of the luxuries of being in Texas? Spring and warm weather comes sooner for us than most of you. No need to be jealous! Come join us for a weekend getaway and enjoy the Texas heat. The pool bar is open every weekend until March 28th, when it will then be open every single day… all summer long. Take a peek at the chef’s menu and pack your suit for some R&R in the Hill Country. more

Farm Fresh Fuel: Just What the Chef Ordered

Not only is it GO Month at Travaasa, but it is also one of the busiest times of year for the Travaasa Farm. Farmer Kim dishes the dirt on the best vegetables to eat this spring for boosting energy levels and shaking the winter blues. Also, find out what happens when a farmer and a chef team up to produce the ultimate Go Fuel… more

Sea Turtles, Whale Watching and Outrigger Canoes

Sea Turtles, Whale Watching and Outrigger Canoes Hawaii’s first residents arrived in paradise via Outrigger Canoes from Polynesia long ago. Taking a page from that history book, Travaasa’s rich cultural programming includes paddling through Hana Bay in our four-person, custom-made outrigger canoe! It’s an interesting twist on a full body workout that may lead you to swimming with sea turtles or watching whales. Make sure you recite the Travaasa Canoe Blessing for good fortune on the high seas… more

Travaasa Spa for the Day

Take advantage of the Full-Day Escape Package and GO explore Travaasa Austin or Hana for the day this month! In Austin: take a yoga class, soar to new heights on the challenge course, ride the mechanical bull, swim in the infinity pool and relax at the spa. In Hana: learn how to throw-net fish, husk a coconut, take a juicing class, get your heart pumping in a cardio challenge, visit the secluded beach and unwind at the spa. Sold? Get your day pass now. more

ON THE HORIZON

April is Grow Month

With spring comes all the things we love most about warmer weather and longer days. We’re looking forward to The Travaasa Farm buzzing with activity once again, as Farmer Kim plants the spring crop. Join us for chicken-keeping classes and delicious farm-to-table meals. The Travaasa Food Truck and blender bike will be making an appearance at the Austin Olive Festival (and you won’t want to miss the olive oil ice cream and cooking demo!). We have a few special celebrations in the works as well! Join us in APRIL.

May is Nurture Month

Before summer heats up and gets underway, take some time to nurture your physical and emotional self during the month of MAY. Both Travaasa Austin and Hana will be celebrating anniversaries, which means you are definitely invited to our party. Travaasa Hana is partnering with Edible Hawaiian Islands to host a dinner benefiting I Am Haloa, a documentary chronicling the journey of three Kamehameha School seniors who commit to cultivating, harvesting and eating taro for three meals a day. The very next day the East Maui Taro Festival kicks off!
