Cabin fever is setting in all over the country. We want to get out, feel the sun on our face, breathe in some warm fresh air. We want to GO.

March is GO Month at Travaasa Experiential Resorts. Go to Austin and eat fresh vegetables from our farm. The team will load your plate with fresh tastes like you’ve never had before. Go to Hana and take a spin in our new outrigger canoe. Multitask by getting a great workout while learning about the history of the Hana Bay, and taking in the Eastern Coast of Maui from a new perspective. It’s something you’ll never forget.

March is a great time to get up and go. And if you go, come to us.
