New Offerings from Travaasa
This Spring, we're all about nature and its many gifts. Travaasa is proud to announce new feature offerings at our Austin and Hana, Maui properties. We hope you enjoy the new experiences as much we did developing them.
FARM YOGA
Have you ever watched the plants grow in standing bow? We didn't think so. Actually, you may be supposed to be looking up during that one... Anyways, we're pretty sure Farm Yoga is gonna be a thing.
FRUIT & VEGGIE MASSAGE
Your fruit and veggies no longer have be jealous of your Kobe beef. Little research has been done on the benefits of massaging your produce, but that's not stopping us. We believe in applying our motto to the food we put in our bodies—treat yo' self!
TREE STRETCH
Anyone can lean against a wall. Conquer your fear of heights, find your balance, your focus, and zen among the strong* branches of Hana's majestic banyan trees.
*Not responsible for not strong branches
UNDERWATER BASKET WEAVING
Learn a classic(?) Hawaiian cultural tradition and discover the value of renewable resources, as well as the value of a good snorkel. Hone your dexterity as you time the waves—just don't let go of your project.
