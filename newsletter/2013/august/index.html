ON THE HORIZON

September

Uncover and discover the best of life at Travaasa Austin and Hana as you step into fall. Try our new outdoor adventure elements that bring more speed and adrenaline to the already exciting High Ropes Course: a BMX Pump Bike Course and a Giant Swing.

For all of our yogis out there, join us as we celebrate National Yoga Month with Chakra-Centered Yoga, Neck and Shoulder Yoga Therapy, Spa Experience Meditation and Yoga Mudras – Honoring the Essentials. Show your competitive side in the Hana Relay, and step up to a cooking challenge in the Mystery Box Challenge in Austin.

October

Recap your best memories during Harvest Month at Travaasa Austin and Hana this October. From Pilates Seeds for Centering to Go Nuts with Coconuts, our classes will put you in the fall mood. There's more to pumpkins than jack-o'-lanterns and Grandma's pies. Learn several delectable ways to prepare pumpkin and squash. Participate in the ultimate autumn activity – Brewing Pumpkins, where can sample seasonal brews and pumpkin ales, and toast to the spirit of the season.

Join us for The Joyful Horse Project Tribute Benefit Concert at Travaasa Austin, October 18, 2013, where Mike Beck will perform an acoustic solo set in support of this equine project and our veterans program.

Aloha Week kicks off October 19 with a parade in Hana. The weeklong festivities continue with live music and entertainment, Hawaiian games, outdoor adventures, cultural tours and much more to celebrate Hawaiian culture in Hana.
