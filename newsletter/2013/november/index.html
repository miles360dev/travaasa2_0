I stayed at The Carneros Inn in Napa, CA a few weeks ago. It was gorgeous. While there, I did something I haven’t done in over 20 years: I didn’t check email or my cell phone for three full days. I STOPPED. It took a little time to unwind, but once my Phantom Vibration Syndrome subsided, I was able to engage and focus on my surroundings in a deeper way.  I was able to relax.  It’s odd to make such a big deal about three days, but how many of us do that anymore?  (I won’t wait two decades before doing it again.)

November is STOP month at Travaasa Experiential Resorts.  Right now, in the middle of annual business plans, holiday travel planning, Black Friday, Cyber Monday, we say STOP.  Take time for yourself or spend time reconnecting with a loved one.  Hit a yoga class.  Get a massage.  Go to Hana and unplug, where the rooms have no TVs or alarm clocks, and you can check your cell phone in to our Cell Phone Hotel.

Come STOP with us at Travaasa this November.  We hope to see you soon.
