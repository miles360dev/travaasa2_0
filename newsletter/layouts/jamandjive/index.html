Pre-Event Info

Thank you for your support of the Sustainable Food Center. We are excited to have to you as a guest for Travaasa Austin and Edible Austin’s Jam & Jive on Saturday, January 25th at Travaasa Austin. We're writing to communicate directions, parking information, and some details on how the workshops and dinner will work.

Directions and Parking

Parking will be at Travaasa Austin with multiple shuttles running in a continuous loop from
2–3:30 p.m., and then again at the end of the night, from 8:30–10:30 p.m. Although we will have shuttles running, the event is a short walk away from the event parking lot.

Travaasa Austin is located at 13500 FM 2769 just Northwest of Austin. Please make your way to the front entrance of the property and enter "the loop" around the property.

If you are just attending the event (not staying at the hotel) event parking is up the hill approximately 0.6 miles from the entrance off 2769. Look for the Jam & Jive "Parking" signage. If you are staying on property, park in normal guest parking.

Shuttles will drop you off on-site at the Welcome Center and event will be held in the Live Oak Center at Travaasa. There will be a person and signage directing you from the Welcome Center to Live Oak Center.

Registration

Registration opens at 2 p.m. and will be located in the foyer of the Live Oak Center. At registration you will select one workshop from each of the two sessions. See below for more detail on the workshops.

The Workshops

We’re bringing you some of the top culinary talent to teach you how to make these specialties in your very own home. Visit travaasa.com/jamandjive for more in-depth descriptions of the workshops.

Workshop Session One | 3:30–4:30 p.m. – choose one

Preserving Austin with Confituras - Jam making workshop with Stephanie McClenny (Workshop Room B)

Sourdough Bread - Fermenting Grains Workshop with Executive Chef Ben Baker (Workshop Room A)

Workshop Session Two | 5:00–6:00 p.m. – choose one

Oh Kimchi! - Ancient art of making Kimchi workshop with Abigail Lunde (Workshop Room B)

Rind To Seed - Citrus preservation workshop with Kate Payne (Workshop Room A)

Social Hour

We will be having a social hour after the workshops with beer, wine and passed apps. Merchandise from the featured talent will be available to purchase during this time. We will also be doing a vintage apron swap during the social hour, so be sure to bring a vintage apron if you would like to participate!

Property Tours

Travaasa will be hosting two optional property tours of the resort throughout the duration of the events on Saturday afternoon/evening. One will be at 2:30 p.m. during registration (so be sure to arrive early if you’d like to participate!) and one will be at 6 p.m. at the start of the social hour.

Schedule of events:

2:00 p.m.    Shuttle service begins / Registration begins
2:30 p.m.    Property Tour 1 begins (optional)
3:30 p.m.    Workshops Round 1
5:00 p.m.    Workshops Round 2
6:00 p.m.    Social Hour begins
6:00 p.m.    Property Tour 2 begins (optional)
6:30 p.m.    Vintage Apron Swap
7:30 p.m.    Dinner service begins
8:30 p.m.    Band begins / Dance Lessons start
10:00 p.m.    Band ends
10:30 p.m.    Shuttle service ends

NOTE: Shuttle service will be running from 2–3:30 p.m. & 8:30–1 p.m. continuously so guests can come and go at will during these times. Although there is shuttle service, the Live Oak Center is also walkable from the parking lot.

Brunch

If you are staying overnight on property, brunch will be available in Jean’s kitchen from
7 a.m. – 1 p.m, along with a tour of the farm at 10:30 a.m. Check-out is at 1 p.m.
